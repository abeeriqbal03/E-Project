Featured Beaches Around the West Zone

Discover the most stunning coastal destinations across western continents

USA

Pfeiffer Beach - USA

Explore Pfeiffer Beach, USA — famous for its purple sand, dramatic rock formations, and the iconic Keyhole Arch along California's Big Sur coast.

Moonstone Beach - USA

Discover Moonstone Beach, USA — a serene California coastline known for moonstone gem hunting, scenic boardwalks, and stunning sunset views in Cambria.

Glass Beach (Fort Bragg) - USA

Visit Glass Beach, USA — where decades of ocean tumbling transformed discarded glass into a sparkling shore of colorful sea glass gems in Fort Bragg, California.

La Jolla Shores - USA

Relax at La Jolla Shores, USA — a family-friendly San Diego beach with gentle waves, tide pools, sea lions, and excellent snorkeling opportunities.

Ruby Beach - USA

Experience Ruby Beach, USA — a rugged Washington coastline featuring towering sea stacks, driftwood-strewn shores, and stunning Olympic Peninsula wilderness.

Shi Shi Beach - USA

Trek to Shi Shi Beach, USA — a remote Washington wilderness beach with dramatic Point of Arches rock formations and pristine Pacific Northwest beauty.

Canada

Rathtrevor Beach - Canada

Explore Rathtrevor Beach, Canada — a stunning Vancouver Island destination with vast sandy shores at low tide and warm shallow waters perfect for families.

Spanish Banks Beach - Canada

Discover Spanish Banks Beach, Canada — Vancouver's expansive sandy shoreline with incredible mountain views, warm tidal pools, and spectacular sunsets.

Kitsilano Beach - Canada

Visit Kitsilano Beach, Canada — Vancouver's vibrant urban beach featuring a saltwater pool, beach volleyball courts, and stunning city skyline views.

Mexico

Sayulita Beach - Mexico

Experience Sayulita Beach, Mexico — a colorful surf town on the Riviera Nayarit with laid-back vibes, excellent waves, and vibrant local culture.

Balandra Beach - Mexico

Discover Balandra Beach, Mexico — a protected paradise near La Paz with shallow turquoise waters, the famous mushroom rock, and pristine white sand.

Playa La Ropa - Mexico

Relax at Playa La Ropa, Mexico — Zihuatanejo's beautiful crescent beach with calm waters, beachfront restaurants, and stunning sunset views.

Chile

Playa Anakena - Chile

Visit Playa Anakena, Chile — Easter Island's most beautiful beach with white coral sand, palm trees, and ancient moai statues overlooking the shore.

Playa Cavancha - Chile

Explore Playa Cavancha, Chile — Iquique's urban beach paradise with golden sands, perfect surf conditions, and a lively waterfront promenade.

Peru

Playa Roja - Peru

Discover Playa Roja, Peru — the stunning Red Beach in Paracas with unique red sand created by volcanic rock and dramatic coastal cliffs.

Punta Hermosa - Peru

Experience Punta Hermosa, Peru — Lima's premier surf destination with consistent waves, sandy beaches, and a relaxed coastal village atmosphere.

Ecuador

Los Frailes Beach - Ecuador

Visit Los Frailes Beach, Ecuador — one of South America's most pristine beaches with crystal-clear waters, protected nature reserve, and untouched beauty.

Canoa Beach - Ecuador

Relax at Canoa Beach, Ecuador — a long stretch of golden sand perfect for surfing, paragliding, and enjoying the laid-back coastal lifestyle.

Ghana

Labadi Beach - Ghana

Experience Labadi Beach, Ghana — Accra's most popular beach with lively music, local food vendors, horseback riding, and vibrant West African culture.

Nigeria

Elegushi Beach - Nigeria

Discover Elegushi Beach, Nigeria — Lagos's trendy beach destination with golden sands, beach parties, water sports, and a vibrant social scene.
