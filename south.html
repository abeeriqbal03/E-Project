Featured Beaches Around the South Zone

Discover the most stunning coastal destinations across southern continents

Australia

Bondi Beach - Australia

Explore the iconic Bondi Beach, Australia — famous for its golden sands, world-class surfing waves, and vibrant coastal culture in Sydney.

Whitehaven Beach - Australia

Discover the pristine Whitehaven Beach, Australia — renowned for its silica sands that squeak underfoot, turquoise waters, and lush rainforest backdrop.

Gold Coast Beach - Australia

Experience the lively Gold Coast Beach, Australia — a stretch of golden sands with thrilling surf spots, theme parks, and endless sunshine.

Cable Beach - Australia

Visit Cable Beach, Australia — known for its red sands, camel rides at sunset, and stunning views of the Indian Ocean in Western Australia.

Hyams Beach - Australia

Relax on Hyams Beach, Australia — celebrated for its powdery white sands, crystal-clear waters, and proximity to Jervis Bay's marine life.

Bells Beach - Australia

Witness the legendary Bells Beach, Australia — a surfing paradise with massive waves, rugged cliffs, and the famous Rip Curl Pro competition.

New Zealand

Piha Beach - New Zealand

Discover Piha Beach, New Zealand — a dramatic black sand beach with powerful surf, rugged landscapes, and stunning Tasman Sea views.

Ninety Mile Beach - New Zealand

Explore Ninety Mile Beach, New Zealand — one of the world's longest beaches, offering endless golden sands, tidal flats, and unique coastal scenery.

Brazil

Copacabana Beach - Brazil

Experience the vibrant Copacabana Beach, Brazil — Rio's famous stretch of sand with mosaic sidewalks, lively atmosphere, and iconic Sugarloaf views.

Ipanema Beach - Brazil

Relax on Ipanema Beach, Brazil — known for its beautiful people, colorful umbrellas, and the birthplace of the bossa nova in Rio de Janeiro.

Lopes Mendes Beach - Brazil

Visit the secluded Lopes Mendes Beach, Brazil — a paradise on Ilha Grande with pristine sands, emerald waters, and lush Atlantic rainforest.

Uruguay

Punta del Este - Uruguay

Discover Punta del Este, Uruguay — a glamorous beach resort with golden sands, luxury hotels, and the stunning meeting point of the Atlantic and Rio de la Plata.

South Africa

Clifton Beach - South Africa

Explore Clifton Beach, South Africa — four pristine coves in Cape Town with white sands, clear waters, and breathtaking views of Table Mountain.

Camps Bay Beach - South Africa

Enjoy Camps Bay Beach, South Africa — a scenic urban beach with palm-lined promenades, vibrant cafes, and the Twelve Apostles mountain range.

Boulders Beach - South Africa

Visit Boulders Beach, South Africa — home to a colony of African penguins, granite boulders, and calm waters near Cape Point.

Seychelles

Anse Source d’Argent - Seychelles

Discover Anse Source d’Argent, Seychelles — famous for its granite boulders, turquoise lagoon, and award-winning beauty on La Digue island.

Beau Vallon Beach - Seychelles

Relax at Beau Vallon Beach, Seychelles — the island's most popular beach with soft white sands, clear waters, and vibrant marine life.

Madagascar

Nosy Be Beaches - Madagascar

Explore Nosy Be Beaches, Madagascar — a collection of stunning shores around the island with pristine sands, coral reefs, and lush tropical landscapes.
