Featured Beaches Around the South Zone

Discover the most stunning coastal destinations across southern continents

Australia

Bondi Beach

Sydney, Australia

Famous for its golden sands, world-class surfing waves, and vibrant coastal culture in Sydney.

Whitehaven Beach

Whitsunday Islands, Australia

Renowned for its silica sands that squeak underfoot, turquoise waters, and lush rainforest backdrop.

Gold Coast Beach

Queensland, Australia

A stretch of golden sands with thrilling surf spots, theme parks, and endless sunshine.

Cable Beach

Western Australia

Known for its red sands, camel rides at sunset, and stunning views of the Indian Ocean.

Hyams Beach

Jervis Bay, Australia

Celebrated for its powdery white sands, crystal-clear waters, and proximity to marine life.

Bells Beach

Victoria, Australia

A surfing paradise with massive waves, rugged cliffs, and the famous Rip Curl Pro competition.

New Zealand

Piha Beach

Auckland, New Zealand

A dramatic black sand beach with powerful surf, rugged landscapes, and stunning Tasman Sea views.

Ninety Mile Beach

Northland, New Zealand

One of the world's longest beaches, offering endless golden sands, tidal flats, and unique coastal scenery.

Brazil

Copacabana Beach

Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Rio's famous stretch of sand with mosaic sidewalks, lively atmosphere, and iconic Sugarloaf views.

Ipanema Beach

Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Known for its beautiful people, colorful umbrellas, and the birthplace of the bossa nova.

Lopes Mendes Beach

Ilha Grande, Brazil

A paradise on Ilha Grande with pristine sands, emerald waters, and lush Atlantic rainforest.

Uruguay

Punta del Este

Maldonado, Uruguay

A glamorous beach resort with golden sands, luxury hotels, and the stunning meeting point of the Atlantic and Rio de la Plata.

South Africa

Clifton Beach

Cape Town, South Africa

Four pristine coves in Cape Town with white sands, clear waters, and breathtaking views of Table Mountain.

Camps Bay Beach

Cape Town, South Africa

A scenic urban beach with palm-lined promenades, vibrant cafes, and the Twelve Apostles mountain range.

Boulders Beach

Simon's Town, South Africa

Home to a colony of African penguins, granite boulders, and calm waters near Cape Point.

Seychelles

Anse Source d'Argent

La Digue, Seychelles

Famous for its granite boulders, turquoise lagoon, and award-winning beauty on La Digue island.

Beau Vallon Beach

Mahé, Seychelles

The island's most popular beach with soft white sands, clear waters, and vibrant marine life.

Madagascar

Nosy Be Beaches

Nosy Be, Madagascar

A collection of stunning shores around the island with pristine sands, coral reefs, and lush tropical landscapes.
