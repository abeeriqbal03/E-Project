Featured Beaches Around the East Zone

Discover the most stunning coastal destinations across eastern continents

Philippines

White Beach (Boracay) - Philippines

Discover White Beach in Boracay, Philippines — a powdery white sand paradise with crystal-clear waters, vibrant nightlife, and endless water sports.

El Nido Beach - Philippines

Explore El Nido Beach, Philippines — surrounded by limestone cliffs, turquoise lagoons, and ideal for island hopping and adventure activities.

Vietnam

Nha Trang Beach - Vietnam

Relax on Nha Trang Beach, Vietnam — known for its pristine sands, vibrant coral reefs, and a mix of relaxation and water-based adventures.

Da Nang Beach - Vietnam

Visit Da Nang Beach, Vietnam — a long stretch of golden sand with modern resorts, Son Tra Peninsula views, and exciting beach activities.

South Korea

Haeundae Beach - South Korea

Experience Haeundae Beach, South Korea — Busan's iconic urban beach with wide sands, luxury hotels, and a lively atmosphere for all seasons.

Jeju Beach - South Korea

Discover Jeju Beach, South Korea — featuring volcanic sands, clear waters, and proximity to Jeju Island's natural wonders and hiking trails.

Japan

Okinawa Beaches - Japan

Explore Okinawa Beaches, Japan — a collection of stunning shores with emerald waters, coral reefs, and a blend of culture and relaxation.

Miyakojima Beach - Japan

Visit Miyakojima Beach, Japan — known for its soft white sands, star-shaped sandbar, and serene island vibes in the Miyako Islands.

Indonesia

Bali (Kuta Beach) - Indonesia

Experience Bali's Kuta Beach, Indonesia — a surfer's paradise with golden sands, vibrant nightlife, and stunning sunsets over the Indian Ocean.

Nusa Dua - Indonesia

Relax at Nusa Dua, Indonesia — Bali's luxury beach area with pristine white sands, calm waters, and world-class resorts and spas.

Gili Islands - Indonesia

Discover the Gili Islands, Indonesia — trio of paradise islands with powdery beaches, crystal-clear lagoons, and opportunities for snorkeling and diving.

Malaysia

Langkawi Beach - Malaysia

Explore Langkawi Beach, Malaysia — featuring soft white sands, duty-free shopping, and access to rainforests and cable car rides.

Perhentian Islands - Malaysia

Visit the Perhentian Islands, Malaysia — a diver's haven with turquoise waters, coral reefs, and secluded beaches for ultimate relaxation.

Thailand

Phuket (Patong Beach) - Thailand

Experience Patong Beach in Phuket, Thailand — a bustling stretch of sand with vibrant nightlife, water sports, and tropical island vibes.

Maya Bay - Thailand

Discover Maya Bay, Thailand — the iconic location from The Beach movie, with emerald waters, limestone cliffs, and pristine natural beauty.

Koh Samui - Thailand

Relax on Koh Samui, Thailand — known for its coconut palms, white-sand beaches, and a mix of luxury resorts and cultural experiences.

China

Sanya Beach - China

Visit Sanya Beach, China — Hainan's tropical paradise with palm-fringed shores, clear waters, and a gateway to China's southernmost resorts.
