Featured Beaches Around the East Zone

Discover the most stunning coastal destinations across eastern continents

Philippines

White Beach (Boracay)

Boracay, Philippines

A powdery white sand paradise with crystal-clear waters, vibrant nightlife, and endless water sports.

El Nido Beach

Palawan, Philippines

Surrounded by limestone cliffs, turquoise lagoons, and ideal for island hopping and adventure activities.

Vietnam

Nha Trang Beach

Khánh Hòa, Vietnam

Known for its pristine sands, vibrant coral reefs, and a mix of relaxation and water-based adventures.

Da Nang Beach

Da Nang, Vietnam

A long stretch of golden sand with modern resorts, Son Tra Peninsula views, and exciting beach activities.

South Korea

Haeundae Beach

Busan, South Korea

Busan's iconic urban beach with wide sands, luxury hotels, and a lively atmosphere for all seasons.

Jeju Beach

Jeju Island, South Korea

Featuring volcanic sands, clear waters, and proximity to Jeju Island's natural wonders and hiking trails.

Japan

Okinawa Beaches

Okinawa, Japan

A collection of stunning shores with emerald waters, coral reefs, and a blend of culture and relaxation.

Miyakojima Beach

Miyako Islands, Japan

Known for its soft white sands, star-shaped sandbar, and serene island vibes in the Miyako Islands.

Indonesia

Bali (Kuta Beach)

Bali, Indonesia

A surfer's paradise with golden sands, vibrant nightlife, and stunning sunsets over the Indian Ocean.

Nusa Dua

Bali, Indonesia

Bali's luxury beach area with pristine white sands, calm waters, and world-class resorts and spas.

Gili Islands

Lombok, Indonesia

Trio of paradise islands with powdery beaches, crystal-clear lagoons, and opportunities for snorkeling and diving.

Malaysia

Langkawi Beach

Langkawi, Malaysia

Featuring soft white sands, duty-free shopping, and access to rainforests and cable car rides.

Perhentian Islands

Terengganu, Malaysia

A diver's haven with turquoise waters, coral reefs, and secluded beaches for ultimate relaxation.

Thailand

Phuket (Patong Beach)

Phuket, Thailand

A bustling stretch of sand with vibrant nightlife, water sports, and tropical island vibes.

Maya Bay

Phi Phi Islands, Thailand

The iconic location from The Beach movie, with emerald waters, limestone cliffs, and pristine natural beauty.

Koh Samui

Surat Thani, Thailand

Known for its coconut palms, white-sand beaches, and a mix of luxury resorts and cultural experiences.

China

Sanya Beach

Hainan, China

Hainan's tropical paradise with palm-fringed shores, clear waters, and a gateway to China's southernmost resorts.
