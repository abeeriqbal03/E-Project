Featured Beaches Around the North Zone

Discover the most stunning coastal destinations across continents

USA

Waikiki Beach - USA

Experience the iconic shores of Waikiki Beach in Hawaii — where golden sands meet crystal-clear waters, perfect for surfing and unforgettable sunsets.

Miami Beach - USA

Dive into the vibrant energy of Miami Beach, Florida — where Art Deco architecture meets turquoise waters and world-class nightlife.

Santa Monica Beach - USA

Discover California's legendary Santa Monica Beach — home to the famous pier, vibrant boardwalk, and endless ocean views.

Clearwater Beach - USA

Relax on the powdery white sands of Clearwater Beach, Florida — consistently rated as one of America's best beaches with stunning Gulf Coast sunsets.

Mexico

Cancun Beach - Mexico

Discover the paradise of Cancun Beach — featuring pristine white sand, turquoise Caribbean waters, and luxurious resorts along the Hotel Zone.

Playa del Carmen - Mexico

Explore Playa del Carmen — a trendy beach town with beautiful shores, vibrant nightlife, and easy access to Mayan ruins and cenotes.

Tulum Beach - Mexico

Visit Tulum Beach — where ancient Mayan ruins overlook stunning Caribbean coastline, offering a perfect blend of history and natural beauty.

Europe

Navagio Beach (Shipwreck Beach) - Greece

Discover the hidden gem of Navagio Beach, Greece — featuring dramatic cliffs, pristine turquoise waters, and the famous shipwreck on its shores.

Myrtos Beach - Greece

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Myrtos Beach, Greece — where white pebbles contrast with brilliant blue waters against towering cliffs.

Elafonissi Beach - Greece

Visit the exotic Elafonissi Beach, Greece — famous for its pink sand created by crushed shells, shallow turquoise lagoons, and stunning natural beauty.

Praia da Marinha - Portugal

Explore Portugal's stunning Praia da Marinha — featuring dramatic limestone cliffs, hidden caves, and some of Europe's clearest waters.

La Concha Beach - Spain

Stroll along La Concha Beach, Spain — an elegant urban beach with golden sand, calm waters, and stunning views of San Sebastián's bay.

Zlatni Rat - Croatia

Visit Croatia's iconic Zlatni Rat — a unique golden cape that shifts with the tides, surrounded by crystal-clear Adriatic waters.

Reynisfjara Black Sand Beach - Iceland

Witness the otherworldly beauty of Reynisfjara, Iceland — where volcanic black sand meets powerful waves and dramatic basalt columns.

Nice Beach - France

Experience the elegance of Nice Beach, France — the famous Promenade des Anglais, stunning French Riviera views, and Mediterranean charm.

Middle East

Jumeirah Beach - Dubai

Indulge in the luxury of Jumeirah Beach, Dubai — where pristine white sand meets turquoise waters with the iconic Burj Al Arab as backdrop.

Corniche Beach - Abu Dhabi

Relax at Corniche Beach, Abu Dhabi — a beautiful urban beach with clear waters, beachfront cafes, and stunning views of the city skyline.

Africa

Hurghada Beach - Egypt

Discover Hurghada Beach, Egypt — a Red Sea paradise offering world-class diving, vibrant coral reefs, and year-round sunshine.

Sharm El Sheikh Beach - Egypt

Experience Sharm El Sheikh Beach, Egypt — renowned for its spectacular underwater world, luxury resorts, and pristine Red Sea coastline.

Agadir Beach - Morocco

Enjoy Agadir Beach, Morocco — a modern resort destination with a long sandy beach, warm climate, and stunning views of the Atlas Mountains.
