Featured Beaches Around the North Zone

Discover the most stunning coastal destinations across continents

USA

Waikiki Beach - USA

Experience the iconic shores of Waikiki Beach in Hawaii — where golden sands meet crystal-clear waters, perfect for surfing and unforgettable sunsets.

Miami Beach - USA

Dive into the vibrant energy of Miami Beach, Florida — where Art Deco architecture meets turquoise waters and world-class nightlife.

Santa Monica Beach - USA

Discover California's legendary Santa Monica Beach — home to the famous pier, vibrant boardwalk, and endless ocean views.

Clearwater Beach - USA

Relax on the powdery white sands of Clearwater Beach, Florida — consistently rated as one of America's best beaches with stunning Gulf Coast sunsets.

Mexico

Cancun Beach - Mexico

Discover the paradise of Cancun Beach — featuring pristine white sand, turquoise Caribbean waters, and luxurious resorts along the Hotel Zone.

Playa del Carmen - Mexico

Explore Playa del Carmen — a trendy beach town with beautiful shores, vibrant nightlife, and easy access to Mayan ruins and cenotes.

Tulum Beach - Mexico

Visit Tulum Beach — where ancient Mayan ruins overlook stunning Caribbean coastline, offering a perfect blend of history and natural beauty.

Europe

Navagio Beach (Shipwreck Beach) - Greece

Discover the hidden gem of Navagio Beach, Greece — featuring dramatic cliffs, pristine turquoise waters, and the famous shipwreck on its shores.

Myrtos Beach - Greece

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Myrtos Beach, Greece — where white pebbles contrast with brilliant blue waters against towering cliffs.

Elafonissi Beach - Greece

Visit the exotic Elafonissi Beach, Greece — famous for its pink sand created by crushed shells, shallow turquoise lagoons, and stunning natural beauty.

Praia da Marinha - Portugal

Explore Portugal's stunning Praia da Marinha — featuring dramatic limestone cliffs, hidden caves, and some of Europe's clearest waters.

La Concha Beach - Spain

Stroll along La Concha Beach, Spain — an elegant urban beach with golden sand, calm waters, and stunning views of San Sebastián's bay.

Zlatni Rat - Croatia

Visit Croatia's iconic Zlatni Rat — a unique golden cape that shifts with the tides, surrounded by crystal-clear Adriatic waters.

Reynisfjara Black Sand Beach - Iceland

Witness the otherworldly beauty of Reynisfjara, Iceland — where volcanic black sand meets powerful waves and dramatic basalt columns.

Nice Beach - France

Experience the elegance of Nice Beach, France — the famous Promenade des Anglais, stunning French Riviera views, and Mediterranean charm.

Middle East

Jumeirah Beach - Dubai

Indulge in the luxury of Jumeirah Beach, Dubai — where pristine white sand meets turquoise waters with the iconic Burj Al Arab as backdrop.

Corniche Beach - Abu Dhabi

Relax at Corniche Beach, Abu Dhabi — a beautiful urban beach with clear waters, beachfront cafes, and stunning views of the city skyline.

Africa

Hurghada Beach - Egypt

Discover Hurghada Beach, Egypt — a Red Sea paradise offering world-class diving, vibrant coral reefs, and year-round sunshine.

Sharm El Sheikh Beach - Egypt

Experience Sharm El Sheikh Beach, Egypt — renowned for its spectacular underwater world, luxury resorts, and pristine Red Sea coastline.

Agadir Beach - Morocco

Enjoy Agadir Beach, Morocco — a modern resort destination with a long sandy beach, warm climate, and stunning views of the Atlas Mountains.

Featured Beaches Around the South Zone

Discover the most stunning coastal destinations across southern continents

Australia

Bondi Beach - Australia

Explore the iconic Bondi Beach, Australia — famous for its golden sands, world-class surfing waves, and vibrant coastal culture in Sydney.

Whitehaven Beach - Australia

Discover the pristine Whitehaven Beach, Australia — renowned for its silica sands that squeak underfoot, turquoise waters, and lush rainforest backdrop.

Gold Coast Beach - Australia

Experience the lively Gold Coast Beach, Australia — a stretch of golden sands with thrilling surf spots, theme parks, and endless sunshine.

Cable Beach - Australia

Visit Cable Beach, Australia — known for its red sands, camel rides at sunset, and stunning views of the Indian Ocean in Western Australia.

Hyams Beach - Australia

Relax on Hyams Beach, Australia — celebrated for its powdery white sands, crystal-clear waters, and proximity to Jervis Bay's marine life.

Bells Beach - Australia

Witness the legendary Bells Beach, Australia — a surfing paradise with massive waves, rugged cliffs, and the famous Rip Curl Pro competition.

New Zealand

Piha Beach - New Zealand

Discover Piha Beach, New Zealand — a dramatic black sand beach with powerful surf, rugged landscapes, and stunning Tasman Sea views.

Ninety Mile Beach - New Zealand

Explore Ninety Mile Beach, New Zealand — one of the world's longest beaches, offering endless golden sands, tidal flats, and unique coastal scenery.

Brazil

Copacabana Beach - Brazil

Experience the vibrant Copacabana Beach, Brazil — Rio's famous stretch of sand with mosaic sidewalks, lively atmosphere, and iconic Sugarloaf views.

Ipanema Beach - Brazil

Relax on Ipanema Beach, Brazil — known for its beautiful people, colorful umbrellas, and the birthplace of the bossa nova in Rio de Janeiro.

Lopes Mendes Beach - Brazil

Visit the secluded Lopes Mendes Beach, Brazil — a paradise on Ilha Grande with pristine sands, emerald waters, and lush Atlantic rainforest.

Uruguay

Punta del Este - Uruguay

Discover Punta del Este, Uruguay — a glamorous beach resort with golden sands, luxury hotels, and the stunning meeting point of the Atlantic and Rio de la Plata.

South Africa

Clifton Beach - South Africa

Explore Clifton Beach, South Africa — four pristine coves in Cape Town with white sands, clear waters, and breathtaking views of Table Mountain.

Camps Bay Beach - South Africa

Enjoy Camps Bay Beach, South Africa — a scenic urban beach with palm-lined promenades, vibrant cafes, and the Twelve Apostles mountain range.

Boulders Beach - South Africa

Visit Boulders Beach, South Africa — home to a colony of African penguins, granite boulders, and calm waters near Cape Point.

Seychelles

Anse Source d’Argent - Seychelles

Discover Anse Source d’Argent, Seychelles — famous for its granite boulders, turquoise lagoon, and award-winning beauty on La Digue island.

Beau Vallon Beach - Seychelles

Relax at Beau Vallon Beach, Seychelles — the island's most popular beach with soft white sands, clear waters, and vibrant marine life.

Madagascar

Nosy Be Beaches - Madagascar

Explore Nosy Be Beaches, Madagascar — a collection of stunning shores around the island with pristine sands, coral reefs, and lush tropical landscapes.

Featured Beaches Around the East Zone

Discover the most stunning coastal destinations across eastern continents

Philippines

White Beach (Boracay) - Philippines

Discover White Beach in Boracay, Philippines — a powdery white sand paradise with crystal-clear waters, vibrant nightlife, and endless water sports.

El Nido Beach - Philippines

Explore El Nido Beach, Philippines — surrounded by limestone cliffs, turquoise lagoons, and ideal for island hopping and adventure activities.

Vietnam

Nha Trang Beach - Vietnam

Relax on Nha Trang Beach, Vietnam — known for its pristine sands, vibrant coral reefs, and a mix of relaxation and water-based adventures.

Da Nang Beach - Vietnam

Visit Da Nang Beach, Vietnam — a long stretch of golden sand with modern resorts, Son Tra Peninsula views, and exciting beach activities.

South Korea

Haeundae Beach - South Korea

Experience Haeundae Beach, South Korea — Busan's iconic urban beach with wide sands, luxury hotels, and a lively atmosphere for all seasons.

Jeju Beach - South Korea

Discover Jeju Beach, South Korea — featuring volcanic sands, clear waters, and proximity to Jeju Island's natural wonders and hiking trails.

Japan

Okinawa Beaches - Japan

Explore Okinawa Beaches, Japan — a collection of stunning shores with emerald waters, coral reefs, and a blend of culture and relaxation.

Miyakojima Beach - Japan

Visit Miyakojima Beach, Japan — known for its soft white sands, star-shaped sandbar, and serene island vibes in the Miyako Islands.

Indonesia

Bali (Kuta Beach) - Indonesia

Experience Bali's Kuta Beach, Indonesia — a surfer's paradise with golden sands, vibrant nightlife, and stunning sunsets over the Indian Ocean.

Nusa Dua - Indonesia

Relax at Nusa Dua, Indonesia — Bali's luxury beach area with pristine white sands, calm waters, and world-class resorts and spas.

Gili Islands - Indonesia

Discover the Gili Islands, Indonesia — trio of paradise islands with powdery beaches, crystal-clear lagoons, and opportunities for snorkeling and diving.

Malaysia

Langkawi Beach - Malaysia

Explore Langkawi Beach, Malaysia — featuring soft white sands, duty-free shopping, and access to rainforests and cable car rides.

Perhentian Islands - Malaysia

Visit the Perhentian Islands, Malaysia — a diver's haven with turquoise waters, coral reefs, and secluded beaches for ultimate relaxation.

Thailand

Phuket (Patong Beach) - Thailand

Experience Patong Beach in Phuket, Thailand — a bustling stretch of sand with vibrant nightlife, water sports, and tropical island vibes.

Maya Bay - Thailand

Discover Maya Bay, Thailand — the iconic location from The Beach movie, with emerald waters, limestone cliffs, and pristine natural beauty.

Koh Samui - Thailand

Relax on Koh Samui, Thailand — known for its coconut palms, white-sand beaches, and a mix of luxury resorts and cultural experiences.

China

Sanya Beach - China

Visit Sanya Beach, China — Hainan's tropical paradise with palm-fringed shores, clear waters, and a gateway to China's southernmost resorts.

Featured Beaches Around the West Zone

Discover the most stunning coastal destinations across western continents

USA

Pfeiffer Beach - USA

Explore Pfeiffer Beach, USA — famous for its purple sand, dramatic rock formations, and the iconic Keyhole Arch along California's Big Sur coast.

Moonstone Beach - USA

Discover Moonstone Beach, USA — a serene California coastline known for moonstone gem hunting, scenic boardwalks, and stunning sunset views in Cambria.

Glass Beach (Fort Bragg) - USA

Visit Glass Beach, USA — where decades of ocean tumbling transformed discarded glass into a sparkling shore of colorful sea glass gems in Fort Bragg, California.

La Jolla Shores - USA

Relax at La Jolla Shores, USA — a family-friendly San Diego beach with gentle waves, tide pools, sea lions, and excellent snorkeling opportunities.

Ruby Beach - USA

Experience Ruby Beach, USA — a rugged Washington coastline featuring towering sea stacks, driftwood-strewn shores, and stunning Olympic Peninsula wilderness.

Shi Shi Beach - USA

Trek to Shi Shi Beach, USA — a remote Washington wilderness beach with dramatic Point of Arches rock formations and pristine Pacific Northwest beauty.

Canada

Rathtrevor Beach - Canada

Explore Rathtrevor Beach, Canada — a stunning Vancouver Island destination with vast sandy shores at low tide and warm shallow waters perfect for families.

Spanish Banks Beach - Canada

Discover Spanish Banks Beach, Canada — Vancouver's expansive sandy shoreline with incredible mountain views, warm tidal pools, and spectacular sunsets.

Kitsilano Beach - Canada

Visit Kitsilano Beach, Canada — Vancouver's vibrant urban beach featuring a saltwater pool, beach volleyball courts, and stunning city skyline views.

Mexico

Sayulita Beach - Mexico

Experience Sayulita Beach, Mexico — a colorful surf town on the Riviera Nayarit with laid-back vibes, excellent waves, and vibrant local culture.

Balandra Beach - Mexico

Discover Balandra Beach, Mexico — a protected paradise near La Paz with shallow turquoise waters, the famous mushroom rock, and pristine white sand.

Playa La Ropa - Mexico

Relax at Playa La Ropa, Mexico — Zihuatanejo's beautiful crescent beach with calm waters, beachfront restaurants, and stunning sunset views.

Chile

Playa Anakena - Chile

Visit Playa Anakena, Chile — Easter Island's most beautiful beach with white coral sand, palm trees, and ancient moai statues overlooking the shore.

Playa Cavancha - Chile

Explore Playa Cavancha, Chile — Iquique's urban beach paradise with golden sands, perfect surf conditions, and a lively waterfront promenade.

Peru

Playa Roja - Peru

Discover Playa Roja, Peru — the stunning Red Beach in Paracas with unique red sand created by volcanic rock and dramatic coastal cliffs.

Punta Hermosa - Peru

Experience Punta Hermosa, Peru — Lima's premier surf destination with consistent waves, sandy beaches, and a relaxed coastal village atmosphere.

Ecuador

Los Frailes Beach - Ecuador

Visit Los Frailes Beach, Ecuador — one of South America's most pristine beaches with crystal-clear waters, protected nature reserve, and untouched beauty.

Canoa Beach - Ecuador

Relax at Canoa Beach, Ecuador — a long stretch of golden sand perfect for surfing, paragliding, and enjoying the laid-back coastal lifestyle.

Ghana

Labadi Beach - Ghana

Experience Labadi Beach, Ghana — Accra's most popular beach with lively music, local food vendors, horseback riding, and vibrant West African culture.

Nigeria

Elegushi Beach - Nigeria

Discover Elegushi Beach, Nigeria — Lagos's trendy beach destination with golden sands, beach parties, water sports, and a vibrant social scene.
